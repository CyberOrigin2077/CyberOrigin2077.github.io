Dataset Overview

Our data includes information from home services, the logistics industry, and laboratory scenarios. Until now, 207224 trajectories have be collected.

Data Collection Platform

The data sources consist of motion capture data of human bodies and RGB-D camera data focused on the head. This combined dataset allows for a comprehensive analysis of human movement and head dynamics, providing both skeletal movement information from motion capture and detailed depth and color data from the RGB-D camera.
